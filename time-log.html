Reflection

Tracking my time was a revealing exercise that exposed inefficiencies in my daily routine. I discovered that unstructured time led to procrastination, particularly during time that I could have allocated as study sessions. Implementing time-blocking transformed my productivity, allowing me to prioritize high-impact tasks like data science coursework. This artifact reflects my growth in self-discipline and critical self-assessment, teaching me to optimize my schedule to balance professional development and personal well-being. These skills will be crucial as I juggle my ongoing data science studies and career goals.
